High and Low Achievers

Highlights the most positive and negative reviews for analysis of feedback and key improvement areas.

SkyReview uses the VADER tool to analyze airline passenger evaluations through sentiment analysis. It performs well for texts containing informal phrases, emoticons, and punctuation. It assigns sentiment values according to positive, negative, and neutral impressions in reviews.

VADER is ideal for texts like social media or customer reviews since it is capable of handling short and often unstructured sentences. It also includes a pre-built lexicon, which saves time and ensures accurate detection of sentiments expressed in customer feedback.

By highlighting customer pain points and areas of excellence, SkyReview empowers airlines to make targeted improvements. From enhancing in-flight amenities to streamlining customer service, the insights enable airlines to stay ahead of competitors.
